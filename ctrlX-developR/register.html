About the Challenge

The ctrlX developR Challenge is a hackathon organized by Bosch Rexroth. The goal of the challenge is to develop innovative solutions using the ctrlX automation platform.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is the ctrlX developR Challenge?

    The ctrlX developR Challenge is a competition for students and young professionals to develop innovative automation solutions using the ctrlX platform.

  • Who can participate in the ctrlX developR Challenge?

    The competition is open to students and young professionals aged 18 to 35.

  • What is the prize for the winning team?

    The winning team will receive a prize of RM 10,000 and the opportunity to showcase their project at an international trade fair.
